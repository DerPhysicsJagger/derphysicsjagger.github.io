About me

My name is Trung Kien Pham, come from Vietnam, you can call me with the nickname "Zinc". I am currently an undergraduate student at Hanoi University of Science & Technology, my major is Aerospace Engineering and I about to take the BSc program at TU Munich in Aerospace, of course. Honest to say, I have a great passion for science, especially Physics. Since high school, I have had the opportunity to participate in some Physics Olympiad Competition and luckily won the second prize in the Vietnamese national physics olympiad (VPhO) and a silver medal in the 2022 European Physics Olympiad. This blog was created to inspire a passion for physics and is mostly about making self-made articles on Physics and Engineering phenomena, so that everyone, including high school students, can approach to General Physics.
